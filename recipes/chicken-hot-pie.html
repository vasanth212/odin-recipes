Chicken hot pie

You can also make a homemade chicken soup with this recipe. Just cut up veggies, shred chicken, and cook in chicken broth.

Ingredients

  • 6 carrots, chopped
  • 6 stalks celery, chopped
  • 1 cup fresh or frozen peas
  • 1 cup fresh or frozen green beans, thawed

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C). Place a baking sheet on the a rack on which you'll bake the pie.
  2. Combine the carrots, celery, peas, green beans, corn, onion, red potatoes, and chicken broth in a large pot.
  3. Place dry chicken gravy mix into another saucepan and gradually whisk in the water until smooth. Bring the mixture to a boil
